Reliability and bug-fix release.

Home screen
- Uninstalling an app now frees its home-screen cells: stale invisible rows no longer block placing, moving or resizing items.
- The notifications widget is added full-width, so its icons stay centered on any column count.
- Drag gestures always release cleanly, cross-surface moves can't be interrupted half-way, and the first-run setup survives an interrupted start.

Weather
- The place name now falls back to a network lookup when the device has no working geocoder (fixes missing city on some Samsung devices), and the cached name expires after a day.

Notifications widget
- Tapping an icon whose app can't be launched now opens its notification settings as intended.

Backup
- Dialogs survive screen rotation, Drive backup asks for authorization before turning on, and items on the last home page restore correctly.

Updater
- The downloaded APK's checksum, size and version are verified before install, and the notification permission is only requested while auto-update is on.

Other
- Calendar refreshes are more robust, search results update live when apps change, and the signal label appears right after granting the phone permission in setup.
